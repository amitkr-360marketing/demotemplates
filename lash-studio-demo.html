How should I prep for my appointment?

Arrive with clean, makeup-free lashes — no mascara or oil-based products for 24 hours. Skip caffeine right before if you can; it makes relaxing under the lamp easier.

How long does a full set take?

Classic sets take about 1.5–2 hours; volume sets 2–2.5. Most clients nap through it.

What's the cancellation policy?

Life happens — just give 24 hours' notice. Same-day cancellations may incur a fee to protect the appointment slot.

How long do extensions last?

With fills every 2–3 weeks, indefinitely. A fresh set typically holds 4–6 weeks as your natural lashes cycle.
