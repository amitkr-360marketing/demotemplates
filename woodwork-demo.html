How a commission works

From sketch to delivery.

STEP 1

The conversation

Tell us the room, the use, the wood you love. We'll talk dimensions and budget honestly.

STEP 2

Sketch & quote

A drawing, a wood sample, and a fixed price. 30% deposit books your slot on the bench.

STEP 3

The build

4–8 weeks of joinery, with photo updates as your piece takes shape.

STEP 4

Delivery day

We deliver, place and level it ourselves — then you live with it for fifty years.
